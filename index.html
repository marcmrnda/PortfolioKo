iSHARE

iSHARE: Student Help and Resource Exchange is a pioneering platform dedicated to the betterment of Filipino students by creating a secure and thriving ecosystem for academic resource sharing and skill-based services. We believe in the transformative power of education and the immense potential residing within the student community. With iSHARE, we aim to provide students with a holistic solution that not only enhances their educational journey but also allows them to leverage their skills and talents for financial support.

LAYA

LAYA, an innovative legal assistance project developed by the iNUvators team from National University - Lipa. LAYA aims to revolutionize legal support in the Philippines by leveraging cutting-edge AI technology to provide free legal guidance and support to all Filipinos. This repository contains the source code and documentation for the LAYA application.

Land of the Rising Sun, Japan

An Unforgettable Tour to the Land of the Rising Sun, Japan is one of my dream countries to visit, and my wish was granted this last year. We roam around the Kansai Region, starting from Osaka, Kyoto, and finally Nagoya. Its a tiring but rewarding kinda trip, the taxi rides are expensive so the only transport is walking and subway, but all of it is worth it especially when it comes food. Tonkatsu, Meat Bun, and my most favorite of all Ramen. One of the best ramen shop, I ever experience trying is the Ichiran Ramen, its a usual ramen but it definitely not normal, the broth is just immaculate and during that time I have a hard time finishing a bowl of ramen but then after I tasted that specific ramen, I never even realized, I gulped it all. This Japan trip is something to remember and one day I hope I will come back and this time I hope God give me a chance to bring the love of my life.
